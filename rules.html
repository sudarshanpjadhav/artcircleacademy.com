Our Education

  • Our Vision

    Art Education will contribute to a more just society and world and students perceive and think in new ways.

  • Our Mission

    Educate Child in the broadest sense,both as creative practitioners engaged with a wide range of disciplines in the visual arts and as enlightened citizens of the world who are prepared to question and transform society.

  • We care

    Kids learns to think creatively, with an open mind. Learns to observe and describe, analyze and interpret. Learns to express feelings, with or without words. Discovers that there is more than one right answer, multiple points of view.
